Campus Life

Primary Wing

i had visited in dec 2018

Primary Wing has classes from 1 - 5. The Primary Wing is located within the campus. It has a reception, playground, assembly area, music room, art room, dance room, emergency room and the classes are located in 3 floors. The classes are well-kept with benches and tables. There are plenty of notice boards all around in the class, in the corridors which makes the environment very appealing.

Senior Wing

For classes 5 - 12

The school has CBSE board. The Senior Wing was huge. It had football ground, cricket ground, squash, basketball ground etc. The auditoruim and the assembly areas were huge. The class rooms occupied 3 floors. Separate rooms for art, music, dance and computers were present as well. The architecture of the Senior Wing was extremely appealing as well.

My Experience

  • Annual Day Function, 2006

    The best part about the annual day functions were the practice sessions we had as the annual day came closer. The sessions included fun and chit-chat with friends. We used to spend the entire day in the dance or the music room. The air had a festive and a carefree aura to it.

  • Music Room Practice

    Among the co-curricular activities the most I participated was in the field of music. I participated in Inter-School Competitions. I also used to be a part of the choir group. The regular music practices was a calming and a soothing experience.There used to be a lot of music workshop that I used to attend even during the holidays.
